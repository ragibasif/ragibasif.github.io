A Farewell

By Harriet Monroe

Good-bye!—no, do not grieve that it is over,

 The perfect hour;

That the winged joy, sweet honey-loving rover,

 Flits from the flower.

Grieve not—it is the law. Love will be flying—

 Yes, love and all.

Glad was the living—blessed be the dying.

 Let the leaves fall.
